About the Sanctuary

Interview Island Wildlife Sanctuary is a pristine protected area located on Interview Island in the Andaman Islands. Established to protect unique flora and fauna, the sanctuary offers an untouched ecosystem ideal for nature lovers and researchers.

Wildlife

The sanctuary is home to feral elephants, wild pigs, and a variety of bird species including the Andaman wood pigeon and the white-bellied sea eagle. Dense tropical forests and coastal ecosystems support an impressive biodiversity.

Visitor Information

The island is accessible by boat with permission from the forest department. Since facilities are minimal, visitors must plan carefully. The best time to visit is between November and March.
